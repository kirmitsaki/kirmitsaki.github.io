Overview

The project

We met with the founders of what was to become Aladdin as the seed of a powerful idea was being conceived whose ultimate aim was to prevent diabetes. Together, through workshops and ongoing collaboration, we have taken this early stage concept and evolved it to what it is today:

A digital healthcare ecosystem that will change the way society approaches healthcare, refocusing the industry toward preventative medicine, where trust and efficiency are at the forefront.

The vision

To improve global disease identification and well-being

Goal 1: Preventative healthcare

Applying cutting edge big data and machine learning technology to mine the wealth of user data to create proprietary preventative care applications.

Goal 2: Healthcare ecosystem infrastructure

To build a blockchain based ecosystem to cut waste and bring transparency and trust to the healthcare sector globally – sharing and automating data and related information.

Goal 3: Healthcare app and marketplace

To enhance the interaction between healthcare providers and patients, as well as offer a convenient route to discounts through the marketplace.

My role

I'm a part of the scrum team responsible for the strategy, design and delivery of the app for both Android and iOS.

I lead the design process, producing all major deliverables and presenting them to the client. Working closely with our PO and developers during our sprint-cycles, I get instant feedback and support to deliver the design stories in time.

The approach

Elemental Concept is working in partnership with Aladdin. Our technologists are using their expertise to:

  • Build the blockchain infrastructure that will enable the ecosystem;

  • Design and build all platforms within the ecosystem;

  • Create the world's largest health data repository, which uses AI and machine learning technology, will facilitate the development of preventative care.

Product discovery

The first step was a series of product discovery workshops, establishing the fundamental concepts of the business problem and scoping the solution. These sessions produced a product story map, defining the key features and functionality of the solution, which was prioritized to deliver an MVP to market quickly and efficiently.

The workshop's success moved us on to the next stage in the evolution of this solution – Business Consultancy.

Business consultancy

To validate the agreed solution, in partnership with Aladdin, our consultants developed the business plan, crafting multiple slide decks to prepare for investor funding. This covered industry, competitor, and technology analysis to ensure we could deliver the right solution to address the problems identified facing the healthcare industry.

UX/UI design

We are design-led from Product Discovery and beyond. The UX team ensures the end-user shapes the design and ongoing development of the product. Our team created prototypes and interface designs for all of the Aladdin platforms and applications.

Product development

With a validated and funded solution, we started the build, adopting lean-agile principles with a scrum team delivering to weekly sprints.

Brand creation

Elemental Concept created the Aladdin brand identity and all associated visual assets, including:

  • Logo and styleguide;

  • Design, build, and content strategy of Bilingual website;

  • Animated explainer video - Scoped, scripted, and visualized in-house.

Methodologies, tools & technologies

Scrum framework & UX Ddsign
Design toolkit

Axure & Overflow - used predominantly for crafting user flow diagrams and technical architecture diagrams.

Xtensio - used for creating user persona diagrams, really useful for PD documentation and templated documents.

Illustrator - proved to be the best tool of choice for prototyping and helped with the initial UI visualisation, iconography creation, creating prototype screens and high-level visuals that can be translated to high-quality prints that were later uploaded on Proto.io to create the high-fidelity prototype. The initial prototype made with Illustrator was not amended after it was delivered to our client. We decided to keep it as it was as a point of reference.

Sketch - When the development started I began using Sketch as I found it stronger in creating consistent and solid UI designs for the mobile app and communicating revisions with the scrum team. The prototype function was a powerful tool in creating transparency in our design process and strengthened our relationship with our client and allowed us to gain feedback and approval from the project's stakeholders.

Zeplin - enabled the sharing of designs with the tech team. It saved my team a lot of time and keeps everything organized in one place. The developers can access the screens and see useful information on the dimensions, typography, colour palette, and all the other components that they need to build fast the product.

Technologies

Security with Blockchain

We are building a blockchain based healthcare ecosystem enabling patients, healthcare professionals and insurers to interact more effectively with each other. The blockchain technology stores the data and provides security to ensure its true independence, permanence, credibility, and ultimate safety.

AI & Machine learning

The data coming in through the Aladdin ecosystem will enable the largest healthcare repository in the world. Deploying machine learning technology on this repository will facilitate advances in preventative care and medical research.

Where we are today

We are currently building the blockchain that will enable the Aladdin ecosystem.

Meanwhile, we are also developing proof of concept prototypes for preventative care, including diabetes retina scanning.

The application

Enables patients and healthcare professionals to easily access, upload and analyse medical records, including images, x-rays, scans, and voice data through an integrated web and mobile ecosystem.
